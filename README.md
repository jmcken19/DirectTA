# TA-Direct

**Centralized communication for students and teaching assistants.**

## Mission

**TA-Direct** offloads academic support from school inboxes to eliminate email clutter. We provide a dedicated portal that prevents TAs from answering the same questions repeatedly and keeps personal school accounts reserved for official university business.

## The Problem

* **Inbox Fatigue**: Important academic queries are buried under administrative noise and automated school notifications.
* **Redundancy**: TAs spend hours answering identical questions across disjointed email threads.
* **Boundary Blur**: Personal school emails become 24/7 help desks, leading to burnout.

## The Solution

* **Email Offloading**: Moves all class-related Q&A out of Outlook/Gmail and into a curated workspace.
* **Query Deduplication**: Flags similar student questions to ensure TAs only answer a specific topic once.
* **Inbox Sanity**: Restores the school email account to its primary purpose: official institutional communication.
