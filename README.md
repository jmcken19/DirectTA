# TA-Direct

**Bridging the Essential Gap Between Students, Professors, and Teaching Assistants.**

In modern higher education, communication bottlenecks choke academic success. Students struggle to reach TAs, professors are overwhelmed with repetitive administrative questions, and Teaching Assistants lack a centralized, secure platform to manage their sections effectively. 

**TA-Direct** is the solution: a premium, high-contrast, fully modular communication portal designed exclusively for the academic triage process.

**To empower Teaching Assistants as the academic frontline, ensuring no student query goes unanswered while fundamentally protecting the TA's time and privacy.**

We believe that university communication shouldn't rely on disjointed email threads, scattered Canvas announcements, or unmanaged Discord servers. TA-Direct provides a curated "Bento Box" portal that creates a seamless, stress-free conduit between the student's need for help and the TA's ability to provide it.

### Core Pillars
1. **The Institutional Gateway**: A secure, school-authenticated nexus that guarantees interactions remain within the academic ecosystem ("C CHARLOTTE").
2. **The Selection Hub**: A streamlined directory allowing students to instantly locate and access their specific TA's portal.
3. **The Bento Portal**: A modular, customizable interface for TAs to centralize their links, office hours, and Live Chat.
4. **The Logic Engine**: An administration powerhouse giving TAs granular control over their availability, visual presence (Aura), and an AI-driven FAQ moderation queue that learns from repetitive questions.

## âœ¨ Features defining the Next-Gen Academic Experience

- **Tech-Noir Minimalism**: A stark, premium Black & White aesthetic utilizing Deep Glassmorphism (`backdrop-blur-md`, `border-white/10`) that removes visual clutter and focuses on utility.
- **Physics-Based Interactions**: Every element, from page loads to button hovers, is driven by Framer Motion spring physics, creating a tactile, responsive environment.
- **Ephemeral Live Chat**: Private, real-time messaging that automatically destructs upon resolution, protecting TA privacy while ensuring immediate student support.
- **AI-Summarized FAQ Engine**: The system actively monitors incoming queries, summarizing repetitive questions into an 'Issue and Fix' format for the TA to approve and publish instantly.
- **Aura Status Toggles**: Visually communicate active "accepting messages" status via customizable, glowing pulse gradients.
